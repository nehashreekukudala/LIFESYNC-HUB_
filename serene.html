Meditation Guide

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit.
  2. Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths.
  3. Focus on your breath, inhaling and exhaling slowly.
  4. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to your breath.
  5. Continue for 5-10 minutes or as long as you feel comfortable.
